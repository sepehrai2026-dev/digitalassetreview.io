Guide

How we evaluate digital assets

Technical architecture

We examine the underlying technology — consensus mechanisms, smart contract design, security audits, and code quality. A protocol is only as strong as its foundation.

Team and governance

Who built it, who controls it, and how are decisions made? We look at team backgrounds, token distribution, DAO governance participation, and treasury management.

Adoption metrics

Real usage matters more than market cap. We track TVL, daily active users, transaction volume, and developer activity to separate genuine traction from vapor.

Risk assessment

Every protocol has risks. We identify smart contract risks, centralization vectors, regulatory exposure, and economic attack surfaces so you can make informed decisions.
